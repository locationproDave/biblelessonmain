# Faith-Based EdTech Sales Representative (Commission + Equity)

## About the Opportunity

We're seeking a passionate, faith-driven sales professional to help bring an innovative AI-powered educational platform to churches, ministries, and Christian educators nationwide. Our SaaS product helps Sunday school teachers and children's ministry leaders create professional, scripture-based curriculum in minutes—a solution that's already gaining traction with churches across the country.

This is a ground-floor opportunity with a fast-growing startup in the faith-based education technology space.

---

- Identify and build relationships with churches, denominations, Christian schools, and ministry organizations
- Conduct product demonstrations and consultations with children's ministry directors, pastors, and education committees
- Develop and execute outreach strategies targeting faith communities
- Attend church conferences, ministry events, and Christian education gatherings
- Build a sustainable pipeline of church partnerships and individual subscribers
- Provide feedback from the field to help shape product development
