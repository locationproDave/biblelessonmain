## Long-Term Profit Participation

### Eligibility Requirements
To qualify for profit participation, rep must:
1. Be active for **12+ consecutive months**
2. Maintain **minimum 10 signups/month average**
3. Customer retention rate **>70%** at 6 months
4. No compliance/conduct issues

### Profit Participation Tiers

#### Tier 1: Revenue Share (Year 1+)
- **1% of revenue** from rep's customer base (ongoing)
- Paid quarterly
- Requires continued minimum activity (5 signups/month)

#### Tier 2: Regional Partner (Year 2+)
- **2% of revenue** from rep's customer base
- **0.5% override** on reps they recruit in their region
- Quarterly profit share from regional growth
- Requires 200+ lifetime signups, 15+/month average

#### Tier 3: Equity Partner (Year 3+ / Exceptional Performance)
- **Equity grant: 0.25% - 1%** (vests over 4 years)
- Offered to top performers who demonstrate:
  - 500+ lifetime signups
  - Consistent 25+/month
  - Leadership in recruiting/training other reps
  - Strategic value (school district connections, denominational relationships)
- Subject to board approval (David & Truman)
